Squeeze-and-Excitation Networks (SE-Net)#

架构概述#

SE-Net由Jie Hu等人于2017年提出，核心思想是通过学习通道间的依赖关系，自适应地重新校准通道特征响应。

SE模块的三个阶段#

1. Squeeze操作#

通过全局平均池化将每个通道的二维特征压缩为一个标量：

\[z_c = \frac{1}{H \times W} \sum_{i=1}^H \sum_{j=1}^W x_c(i,j)\]

其中 \(x_c\) 是第 \(c\) 个通道的特征图，\(H \times W\) 是空间维度。

2. Excitation操作#

通过两个全连接层学习通道间的非线性关系：

\[s = \sigma(W_2 \delta(W_1 z))\]

其中：

  • \(W_1 \in \mathbb{R}^{C/r \times C}\)：降维层，减少参数和计算量

  • \(W_2 \in \mathbb{R}^{C \times C/r}\)：升维层，恢复原始维度

  • \(\delta\)：ReLU激活函数

  • \(\sigma\)：Sigmoid激活函数，输出[0,1]的注意力权重

  • \(r\)：压缩比（通常为16）

3. Scale操作#

将学习到的注意力权重与原始特征图逐通道相乘：

\[\tilde{x}_c = s_c \cdot x_c\]

PyTorch实现#

SE模块实现#
 1import torch
 2import torch.nn as nn
 3import torch.nn.functional as F
 4
 5
 6class SEBlock(nn.Module):
 7    """Squeeze-and-Excitation模块"""
 8    def __init__(self, channels, reduction=16):
 9        super(SEBlock, self).__init__()
10        
11        self.avg_pool = nn.AdaptiveAvgPool2d(1)
12        
13        # 两个全连接层构成瓶颈结构
14        self.fc = nn.Sequential(
15            nn.Linear(channels, channels // reduction, bias=False),
16            nn.ReLU(inplace=True),
17            nn.Linear(channels // reduction, channels, bias=False),
18            nn.Sigmoid()
19        )
20        
21    def forward(self, x):
22        b, c, _, _ = x.size()
23        
24        # Squeeze: 全局平均池化
25        y = self.avg_pool(x).view(b, c)
26        
27        # Excitation: 通过全连接层
28        y = self.fc(y).view(b, c, 1, 1)
29        
30        # Scale: 特征重标定
31        return x * y.expand_as(x)
32
33
34if __name__ == "__main__":
35    # 测试SEBlock
36    x = torch.randn(2, 64, 32, 32)
37    se = SEBlock(64, reduction=16)
38    y = se(x)
39    print(f"Input shape: {x.shape}")
40    print(f"Output shape: {y.shape}")
41    print(f"SEBlock parameters: {sum(p.numel() for p in se.parameters())}")

数学分析#

参数数量#

对于输入通道数 \(C\)，压缩比 \(r\)，SE模块的参数数量为：

\[\text{Params} = \frac{C}{r} \times C + C \times \frac{C}{r} = \frac{2C^2}{r}\]

当 \(C=256, r=16\) 时，参数数量为 \(2 \times 256^2 / 16 = 8,192\)，相对于卷积层的参数可以忽略不计。

计算复杂度#

SE模块的计算开销主要来自全连接层：

\[\text{FLOPs} \approx \frac{2C^2}{r} + C \times H \times W\]

其中 \(H \times W\) 是特征图尺寸。通常SE模块增加的计算开销小于1%。

SE-ResNet集成#

SE-ResNet基础块实现#
 1import torch
 2import torch.nn as nn
 3from .se_block import SEBlock
 4
 5
 6class SEBasicBlock(nn.Module):
 7    """带有SE模块的ResNet基础块"""
 8    expansion = 1
 9    
10    def __init__(self, inplanes, planes, stride=1, downsample=None, reduction=16):
11        super(SEBasicBlock, self).__init__()
12        
13        # 第一个卷积层
14        self.conv1 = nn.Conv2d(inplanes, planes, kernel_size=3, 
15                               stride=stride, padding=1, bias=False)
16        self.bn1 = nn.BatchNorm2d(planes)
17        
18        # 第二个卷积层
19        self.conv2 = nn.Conv2d(planes, planes, kernel_size=3,
20                               stride=1, padding=1, bias=False)
21        self.bn2 = nn.BatchNorm2d(planes)
22        
23        # SE模块
24        self.se = SEBlock(planes, reduction)
25        
26        # 下采样和激活函数
27        self.downsample = downsample
28        self.stride = stride
29        self.relu = nn.ReLU(inplace=True)
30        
31    def forward(self, x):
32        identity = x
33        
34        out = self.conv1(x)
35        out = self.bn1(out)
36        out = self.relu(out)
37        
38        out = self.conv2(out)
39        out = self.bn2(out)
40        
41        # 应用SE模块
42        out = self.se(out)
43        
44        if self.downsample is not None:
45            identity = self.downsample(x)
46            
47        out += identity
48        out = self.relu(out)
49        
50        return out
51
52
53if __name__ == "__main__":
54    # 测试SEBasicBlock
55    x = torch.randn(2, 64, 32, 32)
56    block = SEBasicBlock(64, 64)
57    y = block(x)
58    print(f"Input shape: {x.shape}")
59    print(f"Output shape: {y.shape}")
60    print(f"Block parameters: {sum(p.numel() for p in block.parameters())}")
